Help Center

Getting Started

Navigate to the Appointment section from the sidebar, browse available doctors, select your preferred doctor, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking.

Click the edit icon on your dashboard's health information card. You can update your weight, height, blood group, and other health metrics.

Medical Records

Click the "Upload" button in the Medical Records section, select your file (PDF, JPG, or PNG), add a description, and click upload. Your documents are securely stored and accessible anytime.

For security and compliance reasons, medical records cannot be deleted by users. Please contact support if you have concerns about specific documents.

AI Health Assistant

The Health Assistant uses AI to analyze your symptoms and recommend appropriate specialists. Simply describe your symptoms in the chat, and it will provide guidance and suggestions.

Chat history is stored locally in your browser session. You can clear it anytime by clearing your browser data.
