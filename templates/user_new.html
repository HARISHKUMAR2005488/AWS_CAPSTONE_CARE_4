Help Center

Getting Started

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, navigate to the Appointment section from the sidebar, browse available doctors, select your preferred doctor, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking.

How do I update my health information?

Click the "Edit Info" button on your dashboard's health data card. You can update your weight, height, blood group, and other health metrics.

Medical Records

How do I upload medical documents?

Go to the Records section, click "Upload Document", select your file (PDF, JPG, or PNG), add a description, and click upload. Your documents are securely stored and accessible anytime.

Can I delete my medical records?

For security and compliance reasons, medical records cannot be deleted by users. Please contact support if you have concerns about specific documents.

Health Assistant

How does the AI Health Assistant work?

The Health Assistant uses AI to analyze your symptoms and recommend appropriate specialists. Simply describe your symptoms in the chat, and it will provide guidance and suggestions.

Is my chat history saved?

Chat history is stored locally in your browser session. You can clear it anytime by clicking the trash icon in the chat header.
